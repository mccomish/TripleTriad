    How to Play

    The Basics

    Triple Triad is a card game played on a 3×3 grid. Each player picks 5 cards from their collection to form a deck, then takes turns placing cards on the board.

    Card Values

    Every card has 4 numbers — one on each edge: Top, Right, Bottom, Left. Higher numbers are stronger. Card levels range from 1 (common) to 10 (legendary).

    Capturing Cards

    When you place a card next to an opponent's card, the touching edges are compared. If your number is higher, you capture their card and it turns to your color.

    For example: if you place a card with a 7 on its left edge next to an opponent's card that has a 3 on its right edge, you capture it.

    Winning

    Once all 9 spaces are filled, the player who owns more cards on the board (plus cards left in hand) wins. The winner gets to claim one card from the loser's deck!

    Coins & Packs

    You earn coins after every match — even losses! Spend coins in the Shop to buy card packs and grow your collection. Higher-tier packs have a better chance of rare cards.

    Auction House

    List your extra cards for sale at any price, or browse what other players are selling. It's a great way to get the cards you need!

    Tips

    • Place cards in corners to protect two edges from attack.
    • Save your strongest cards to flip the board late in the game.
    • Play against the AI to practice and earn coins risk-free (no card claim).
    • Duplicate cards can be sold for coins or listed on the Auction House.
